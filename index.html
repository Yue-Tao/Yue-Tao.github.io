Brief Introduction

  • Full Professor, the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University (since 2023)
  • Chief Research Scientist, Simula Research Laboratory, Oslo, Norway, (until 2023)
  • Ph.D. on Software Engineering from Carleton University, Ottawa, Canada, (2006-2010)

Research Interests

All of Tao Yue’s research contributions are fundamentally rooted in basic research within the field of software engineering. - Software Testing - Quantum software engineering - Digital twin technologies - Uncertainty-wise software engineering - Model-based engineering (MBE) and model-based system engineering (MBSE) - Search-based software engineering - Empirical Software Engineering
